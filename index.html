LORE

"I will bury the world in ice."

- Lissandra

In a time long forgotten, before the sands birthed and then swallowed Shurima, beings of old magic freely walked Runeterra. The borders between the mortal realm and what lay beyond it were hotly contested.

It was Lissandra who stood against the wild magic of the mortal world itself. For this defiance, the savage claws of a primal god raked across her eyes, blinding her. Though each sister had lost a part of themselves, it was on the frozen fields of Lissandra’s many battles that they were able to unite and prevail. Together, they were unstoppable… but even a bond of blood could only weather so much.

As the reclusive leader of the Frostguard, many believe Lissandra is a living saint whose followers bring healing and wisdom to the tribes of the Freljord. The truth is perhaps more sinister, as she uses her elemental magic to twist the power of True Ice into something dark and terrible, entombing or impaling any who would reveal her deepest secrets. Indeed, the legacy of her past may yet be the beginning of the end for Runeterra.

WHY YOU SHOULD USE LISSANDRA?

If you like badass ice-magic, Elsa of Garendel but better, and a villain that you can root for, then Lissandra is the perfect champion for you. Her intelligence combined with her beauty and elegance is aesthetically pleasing. Her character design is sublime and has a passive that will turn dead bodies into walking detonating bombs.

Lissandra provides great crowd control thanks to her passive, Q, W and R that helps her to look some picks which can be easily followed with her teammates. She can initiate fights and burst priority targets with her combos. As of patch 13.06, her win rate is 49.97 % which is a pretty solid winrate for a mid laner. She can be blind picked and is very good counter to meta-assassins of the patch at the moment especially to Zed, Fizz, Talon, Leblanc and Qiyana.
